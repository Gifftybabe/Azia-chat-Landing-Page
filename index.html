How It Works

Step 1: Sign Up

Create your account by providing your name, email, and password. You can also choose a unique username for identification.

Step 2: Find Friends

Search for friends by their usernames or import contacts from your address book. Connect with people you know or discover new connections.

Step 3: Start Conversations

Initiate conversations by selecting a friend from your contacts or create group chats for team collaboration or shared interests.

Step 4: Engage and Collaborate

Send text messages, share photos and videos, and express yourself with emojis. Collaborate in real-time with seamless file sharing and voice/video calls.
